About Me

In order to stay true to myself and my story, I cannot begin this without mentioning my hardships. I come from an underprivelaged background where my ancestors experienced cultural displacement, religious wars, and trauma for generations. They survived with scars that cannot be seen or measured. These scars were passed on to me through violence, neglect, and abuse.

Breaking the cycle of generational suffering has come with its own challenges. Being born in this reality drives my passion for uplifting marginalised communities, equity, and for mental health awareness. I studied Gender Studies and Psychology at The University of Auckland and worked in the Mental Health space for over 7 years.

One day, I realised that supporting rangatahi in an industry that was not supporting me while I sacrificed my need for a better quality of life is also an aspect of that old cycle for me. I decided to pursue something that breaks through internalised ideas regarding my capabilities while also enhancing my quality of life- Software Development!
