Learning Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

Asking for help and accepting that help! I am more than happy to help others and give my time to them but I struggle to ask for help and for others' time.
A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme. This should include: An explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators and industry/community representatives What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you

If things are starting to build up for me, I will contact my facilitators for support. I will also have my support network involved.

I expect to have semi-regular contact with my facilitators and for them to be responsive and supportive.
